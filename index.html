About me

Hello, I'm Marius, a 17 years old self-taught Web Designer and Graphic Designer.

I've started with Web Design at the age of 16 years old, and with Graphic Design, especially working in Adobe Photoshop, at the age of 12 years old.

I spend most of my time learning about React JS, practicing through creating a lot of small projects, learning small things about it that could help me in the future.

I'm very excited about the frameworks, technologies, and basically all the things that are used in Web Development, hoping and expecting to learn as much things as possible about this domain.

My free time is spend reading, an activity I consider crucial that involves my thought process, which is probably one of the most important conditions when it comes to coding and creating. I state these things after experiencing everything on myself.
